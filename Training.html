What We Teach in Our Training Academy

Introduction to Tattoo Art

Our training academy provides a comprehensive introduction to tattoo art, including fundamental techniques, safety practices, and the essentials of tattoo design. Perfect for beginners looking to start their journey in the tattoo industry.

This course aims to build a strong foundation in understanding skin anatomy, ink properties, and basic machine handling, ensuring that you start your tattoo career with confidence and skill.

Advanced Tattoo Techniques

For those who have mastered the basics, our advanced classes dive into complex techniques, including shading, color theory, and custom designs. Learn from experts to refine your skills and elevate your artistry.

The course also includes in-depth workshops on tattoo realism, portraiture, and how to effectively incorporate multiple techniques to create unique and personalized tattoo designs for clients.

Specialty Styles

Explore various tattoo styles such as traditional, neo-traditional, tribal, and realistic tattoos. Our courses are designed to expand your versatility and help you find your unique artistic voice in the tattoo world.

Students are encouraged to experiment with different styles under the guidance of experienced mentors, helping them to develop a unique style that will set them apart in the tattoo industry.

Business and Hygiene Practices

Our training also covers essential aspects of running a tattoo business, including client consultation, setting up a studio, hygiene standards, and legal considerations. This ensures that our students are well-prepared to handle the business side of tattooing.

We emphasize proper sterilization techniques, equipment handling, and developing customer service skills that are vital for maintaining a successful and reputable tattoo studio.
